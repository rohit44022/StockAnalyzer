Triple Conviction Engine

Combines Bollinger Bands (I–IV), Murphy's Technical Analysis, and Al Brooks Price Action into a single maximum-conviction system.

BB tells you WHAT is happening · TA tells you WHY · PA tells you HOW — all three agreeing = highest probability

🧠 Why Three Independent Systems?
Most traders rely on a single approach. This engine runs 3 independent analysis frameworks simultaneously: Bollinger Bands (volatility & squeeze mechanics from John Bollinger), Technical Analysis (trend, momentum, volume, patterns from John Murphy), and Price Action (bar-by-bar structure from Al Brooks).

Each system scores -100 to +100 independently. When all three reach the same conclusion, the probability of a correct signal increases significantly. When they disagree, it's a warning to stay cautious. The combined score (max ±390) with cross-validation gives you the most complete picture possible.

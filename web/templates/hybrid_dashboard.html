Hybrid Conviction Engine

Combines Bollinger Band Methods (I–IV) with Murphy's Technical Analysis Framework for maximum trading conviction.

🧠 What is the Hybrid Conviction Engine?
Most traders use EITHER Bollinger Bands OR Technical Analysis indicators. This engine uses BOTH systems simultaneously and checks if they agree or disagree. Think of it like getting a second medical opinion — when two independent systems reach the same conclusion, your confidence in that conclusion should be much higher.

How it works: We run all 4 Bollinger Band methods (Squeeze, Trend Following, Reversals, Band Walking) and score them out of 100 points. Then we run Murphy's Technical Analysis (Trend, Momentum, Volume, Patterns, Support/Resistance, Risk) and score that out of 100 points. Finally, we cross-validate — if both agree, we add conviction bonus. If they disagree, we reduce conviction and warn you to wait. The result is a combined score out of 200+ that tells you how confident we are in the trade.
